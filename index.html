About Us

Stats-O-Locked is a DSW-approved, student-led technical club of VIT Bhopal University, focused on Statistics, Data Science, and Machine Learning. We strive to build analytical and problem-solving skills through hands-on learning, collaborative activities, and industry-aligned initiatives.

👁️

Our Vision

To build a thriving community of data-driven thinkers and innovators at VIT Bhopal — empowering students with the analytical, technical, and leadership skills needed to solve real-world problems through statistics, data science, and machine learning.

🎯

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical applications by organizing workshops, hackathons, data challenges, expert talks, competitions, and real-world data-driven projects — with a strong emphasis on team-based learning, leadership development, and collaborative growth.

🚀

Objectives

  • Organize workshops, hackathons, data challenges, and technical competitions.
  • Facilitate expert talks and industry-aligned, real-world data projects.
  • Support VIT Bhopal in academic & technical events with trained volunteers.
  • Collaborate with universities, clubs & professional organizations for cross-institutional learning and peer networking.
  • Promote industry exposure and practical skill development in ML & Data Science.
